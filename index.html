About

What makes me, me?

I'm a Computer Science graduate from the University of Calgary passionate about solving complex problems with big impact. My most recent tenure has been with Amazon where I worked in the Selling Partner Financial Tech organization. There, I helped provide sellers with a set of highly available, scalable services for tax storage and collection.

Prior to Amazon, I worked at General Dynamics Mission Systems-Canada where I delivered integrated solutions to the Canadian Army for keeping and validating all inventory according to NATO specifications. I also was grateful to gain experience in the form of an internship there, where I learned about scalable disctributed systems.

I think the most important thing that makes me, me is my passion for everything that I do. I'm a highly analytical person who also has the abilty to see beyond the surface into more philosophical realms.

People who know me best would describe me as, "creative, meticulous, intelligent, confident and funny". I'm a visual person who loves the arts; especially, photography. Big fan of music and a bit of a shower singer myself; that occasionally, spills over to the stage.

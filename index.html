About

What makes me, me?

That's a pretty interesting question actually. Let me start off by covering the basics. I'm a computer science student at the University of Calgary and co-founder of a non-profit organization called, The Uplyft youth association for domestic abuse awareness where I held the role of Director of events and volunteers for 2 years.

Now into the more abstract bits, I think the most important thing that makes me, me is my passion for everything that I do. I like to think that I'm a very analytical person who also has the abilty to see beyond the surface into more philosophical realms.

Hoping that you aren’t bored yet, I’m gonna talk a bit about my characteristics and hobbies. I've been described as, "creative, meticulous, intelligent, confident, and funny" by people who know me best. Hobbies, are a bit more complex. Learning about physics and how nature works has always been a passion of mine. I'm a visual person and love the arts; photography especially. Big fan of music and a bit of a shower singer myself; that occasionally spills over to the stage.

About

What makes me, me?

I'm a Computer Science graduate from the University of Calgary passionate about solving complex problems with big impact. My most recent tenure has been with Amazon where I worked in the Selling Partner Financial Tech organization. There, I helped provide sellers with a set of highly available, scalable solutions for tax storage and collection.

Prior to Amazon, I worked for General Dynamics where I was responsible for delivering integrated solutions to the Canadian Army for keeping and validating all inventory according to NATO specifications. I also was grateful to gain experience in the form of an internship there, where I learned about scalable disctributed systems.

I think the most important thing that makes me, me is my passion for everything that I do. I'm a highly analytical person who also has the ability to see beyond the surface into more philosophical realms.

I've been described as, "creative, meticulous, intelligent, confident and funny" by people who know me best. I'm a visual person and love the arts; photography especially. Big fan of music and a bit of a shower singer myself; that occasionally spills over to the stage.
